Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Your Privacy Matters: Tree of Life Health respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal and health information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Date of birth
  • Location (optional)
  • Password (encrypted)

Health Information

  • Medical conditions and history
  • Medications and supplements
  • Symptoms and health concerns
  • Constitutional assessments (dosha, etc.)
  • Lifestyle information (diet, exercise, sleep)
  • Treatment tracking and outcomes

Usage Information

  • Conversations with AI assistant
  • Features used and frequency
  • Device information
  • Log data and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized health guidance
  • Power our AI assistant with relevant context
  • Track your health progress over time
  • Improve our services and features
  • Communicate with you about your account
  • Ensure platform security and safety

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted at rest and in transit
  • HIPAA Compliance: Health data handled according to HIPAA standards
  • Access Controls: Strict limits on who can access your data
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and updates

4. Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal or health information.

We may share limited data with:

  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, and support services (under strict contracts)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your health data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications

To exercise these rights, email: privacy@treeoflifehealth.com

6. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Inactive accounts: Data deleted after 12 months of inactivity (with warning)
  • Deleted accounts: 30-day grace period, then permanent deletion
  • Anonymized data: May be retained for research and improvement

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information:

  • Anthropic Claude: AI conversation processing
  • Analytics: Usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Payment Processing: Secure payment handling

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18 without parental consent.

10. International Users

Your information may be processed in the United States. We comply with applicable data protection laws including GDPR for EU users.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify you of material changes via email or prominent notice on our platform.
