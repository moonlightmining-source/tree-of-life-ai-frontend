Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 8, 2024

Introduction

Tree of Life AI ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Name
  • Date of birth
  • Sex/gender
  • Location (ZIP code)

Health Information:

  • Current health conditions
  • Medications and supplements
  • Allergies
  • Medical history
  • Constitutional types (dosha, TCM patterns)
  • Lifestyle information (diet, exercise, sleep)
  • Symptom tracking data
  • Treatment tracking data

Conversation Data:

  • Messages you send to the AI
  • Health questions and concerns
  • Progress notes

Usage Data:

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Pages visited
  • Features used
  • App crashes and errors

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Create your account, provide personalized health guidance, track progress
  • Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
  • Communicate: Send notifications, respond to inquiries, provide support
  • Ensure Safety: Detect emergencies, prevent fraud, protect unauthorized access

How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Render), AI services (Anthropic Claude), analytics, payment processing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights/safety
  • Business Transfers: In event of merger or acquisition (with notice)
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly approve sharing

Data Security

We implement strong security measures:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access, multi-factor authentication
  • HIPAA-Compliant Infrastructure: Secure, compliant cloud hosting
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and penetration testing

However, no method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@treeoflifeai.com

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Retained while account is active
  • Inactive accounts: Data deleted after 12 months with warning
  • Deleted accounts: 30-day grace period, then permanent deletion
  • Anonymized data: May be retained for research

Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18 without parental consent (family plans require parent/guardian management).

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to know what information we collect, use, and share
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (Note: We do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EEA residents have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and objection. Contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@treeoflifeai.com

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Session management
  • Remembering preferences
  • Analytics (with consent)

You can control cookies through browser settings.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
